Refund Policy

At Infinitum Tech, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction with all our products and services. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are issued.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are granted under the following circumstances:

  • Product/service malfunction that is reported within 14 days of purchase.
  • Incorrect product/service delivered.

2. Non-Refundable Items

Certain products and services may not be eligible for refunds. Please review the specific product/service terms for details.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our support team at infinitumtech@outlook.com. Provide your order number and a description of the issue.

4. Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days. The credited amount may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
